Flash Distillation (Ethanol and Water)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Click on "Inlet" button to inlet the feed of Ethanol and Water binary mixture to Flash drum.

  2. Click on "Start" button th start the flash distillation process.Wait till smoke comes out.

  3. The process is done under constant pressure of 1 atm or 101.3kPa with increasing temperature(upto 100℃)

  4. Aftr the smoke comes out click on "Y-X Diagram" under "Plot" button to observe the McCabe-Thiele diagram for the EtOH-water binary mixture.This is the equilibrium curve.

  5. Click on 'TX-Y Diagram' under "Plot",to obser the T vs. X and T vs. Y curve for EtOH-water binary mixture.Also note the 'Bubble Point' temperature.
